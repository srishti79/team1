PLAYING THE GAME FAQ's

HOW DO I PLAY THE FANTASY CRICKET GAME ?
Please follow these simple steps to play the world’s largest Fantasy Cricket game of skill and win cash legally!
1 - Register / Log in to PlayerzPot.
2 - Select a match and then it will redirect you to create Squad page once you clicked on create Squad.
3 - Select your Fantasy Squad of 11 Players (including a Star Player and Captain) within the virtual budget of 1000 Gems from all the Players in the particular match.
4 - Join Paid or Free Pots of your choice. Paid Pots let you win Cash and require an entry fee, for which we provide payment methods such as citrus wallet.
5 - Once the Live match starts, your Fantasy Squad starts earning points on the basis of actual performance of the Players selected by you. Final points, rankings and winners are declared after the end of the match.
WHEN DOES THE GAME STARTS?
The game starts with the actual timing of the match. I.e. prior to the first bowl of the match.
HOW DO I CREATE A SQUAD?
Select any Upcoming match which is not locked then select create squad and Create a Squad. For rules regarding the Squad selection visit our How to play page.
CAN I CHANGE MY FANTASY CRICKET SQUAD FOR FUTURE ROUNDS?
Yes, you are required to create a new Fantasy Cricket squad for every round on PlayerzPot.
CAN I MAKE CHANGES IN THE SQUAD AFTER I ENTER A POT?
Yes, you can make changes in your Squad until the pot is locked. Once the pot is locked you cannot make changes in your Squad. The pot is locked before the first bowl of the official match is bowled or with the official match beginning time.
CAN I SET THE FIELDING POSITIONS?
No , The fielding positions are set and cannot be changed by any of the users.
HOW CAN I CHANGE MY STAR PLAYER OR CAPTAIN?
By Clicking on Manage Squad, you can change your Star Player or Captain.
CAN I MAKE CHANGES IN MY SQUAD AFTER THE MATCH HAS BEGUN?
No. You can make changes to your squad before the pot is locked.
WHAT IF PLAYER OF MY SQUAD IS INJURED DURING THE MATCH?
Player receives points based on his performance. If he is injured during the match so from the time he is injured till his return on the ground for that same match no points will be allotted to that Player.
WHEN CAN I SEE MY OPPONENTS SQUAD?
When all the pots are locked you can see your opponents Squad.
HOW MANY PLAYERS WILL BE THEIR IN A SQUAD?
Eleven no less no more for valid squad.
WHAT WILL BE THE AMOUNT OF GEMS PROVIDED FOR CREATING A SQUAD?
1000 Gems no less no more. Every user will get same amount of gems for all matches irrespective of the pot he joins.
WHAT ARE THE ROLES OF STAR PLAYER AND CAPTAINS?
Star Player receives x2 points for that particular match and the Captain receives x1.5 of his points.
WHAT IF MY SELECTED PLAYER IS NOT PLAYING THE MATCH?
He will not receive any points for that match.
WHAT IF MY STAR PLAYER OR CAPTAIN IS NOT PLAYING THE MATCH?
He will not receive any points for that match.
HOW MANY PLAYERS CAN I SELECT FROM THE SAME SQUAD?
7 at Max.
WHAT IF THE BUDGET OFFERED TO ME IS FINISHED BEFORE COMPLETING THE SQUAD?
It is mandatory to create a Squad in the budget you are given, no extra gems will be provided in this case.
WHAT ARE THE MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS TO MAKE A SQUAD ELIGIBLE TO ENTER A POT?
You can Check on How to Play for all details.

SCORES & POINTS FAQ's

HOW DO I SCORE POINTS?
You will score points depending on how the cricketers in your PlayerzPot Squad perform in the live match(es). So make sure you pick a winning combination to help you win cash. Please check our ‘Points System’ in the ‘How to Play’ section to understand the details.
WHEN WILL THE SCORES WILL BE UPDATED?
Fantasy Cricket Points are usually updated every few minutes! The final points including your Squad's total and your rank are updated once the round gets over. Winners are declared and Winnings are distributed only after our Squad verifies the points.
I HAVE CREATED MY FANTASY CRICKET SQUAD BEFORE THE DEADLINE SO WHY HASN'T MY FANTASY CRICKET SQUAD GOT ANY POINTS AS YET?
Fantasy Cricket Game points are awarded only when the live match begins.
WHICH WEBSITE DO YOU REFER TO UPDATE THE FANTASY CRICKET POINTS?
We get our Live Score feeds from Various online score update sites to make sure we get accurate scores. All our Fantasy Cricket Game's points are typically calculated using these live feeds from various sources.
WILL I GET POINTS FOR MAN OF THE MATCH?
There are no points to be earned for Man of the Match.
WHAT HAPPENS WHEN THERE IS A TIE BETWEEN USERS? HOW WILL YOU DISTRIBUTE THE PRIZE?
If there is a tie between 2 or more users, then the prize money is divided equally between the users.
DO I GET POINTS FOR THE SUPER OVER IN A MATCH?
No! We do not consider the Super Over in our Fantasy Cricket Game. The runs/wickets scored by the cricketers are not considered in their official records; hence the Fantasy Cricket Game also does not consider these points.
HOW DO I CROSS CHECK MY FANTASY CRICKET SQUAD’S POINTS?
Click on view all match history , then select the match of which you want to know scores of and then click on Team Score.
DO THE GEMS ASSIGNED TO PLAYER CHANGES?
Yes, They change according to the performance of the particular Player.
HOW DO YOU CALCULATE FORM OF A PLAYER?
Considering the Players performance in his last five matches.
HOW ARE BONUS POINTS CALCULATED?
Information about bonus is been provided in the how to play section.
WHEN ARE BONUS POINTS ADDED?
While the match is ongoing as the Players scores are updated the bonuses are also added.
WHAT IF CAPTAIN GET DECIMAL NUMBER?
If captain earns total points in decimal number then we select the upper value of the point.
For EX: If captain receives 13.5 points we consider it as 14 point.

POT FAQ's

WHY WAS THE WINNING AMOUNT WAS REDUCED?
The winning amount will be reduced when that pot is not filled. The match will start who has entered in that pot within them.
WHY WAS THE POT PLAYER SIZE REDUCED?
Pot Player size reduce when that pot is not fully filled till Game Starts.
WHAT TYPE OF POTS CAN I PARTICIPATE IN?
You can join a host of Pots like 2 members, 3 members, 5 members and 10 members Pots (depending on the number of competitors in a Pot).You can join paid Pots to win Cash and / or the Strategy Pots to enhance your Fantasy Cricket skills till you are confident with your selection skills to enter the Paid Pots to win Cash.
HOW DO I JOIN POTS?
In order to join Pots, please click on the ‘Join Now’ button below the specific Pot you wish to join.
WHAT ARE PAID & SKILL POTS?
Paid Pots are where user participate by paying an entry fee and compete against each other to win Cash! The prize money will be awarded to the Player who has the highest Fantasy Cricket Points in the Pot. In a Skill Pot, users compete against each other for skills and XP's. Unlike the paid Pots, there will be no entry fee for this Pot. This Pot will help you boost your Fantasy Cricket Skills. Once you are confident with your selection skills, you can enter the paid Pots and win Cash.
HOW DO I KNOW IF A POT HAS A SINGLE WINNER OR MORE THAN ONE WINNER?
Whenever a pot has more than one winner the pot is multiple winners pot. Click on the pot to know the winning bifurcation.
WHAT DO I WIN IN A STRATEGY POT?
Strategy Pots do not have any Cash prizes and are meant for users who want to gain experience in playing and boost their Fantasy Cricket gaming skills.
WHAT DO I WIN IN A PAID POT?
In a Paid Pot, you will win Cash!
WHY DO RANKS KEEP FLUCTUATING DURING A POT?
As the cricketers are playing in the real-life match, your Fantasy Cricket Squad keeps earning points for your chosen cricketer’s performances. The point status appearing next to your total points, will indicate if the round is in progress or completed.
CAN I JOIN FUTURE ROUNDS AND POTS IN ADVANCE?
Yes.
CAN I VIEW OTHER USERS' FANTASY CRICKET SQUAD(S) IN THE COMPETITION?
Yes! But in order to have some suspense, we allow users to view other users’s Fantasy Cricket Squad(s) only after the deadline hits. To view your opponent’s Squad, go to the Joined Pots tab for a particular round and click on your opponent’s Squad name. You can also track your competition using our 'Competitor Tracker' feature and download the details of other Squads in that Pot.
HOW DO YOU DECIDE THE WINNER OF A POT?
The Fantasy Cricket Squad which gets the highest points at the end of the round will be announced as the winner of that Pot.
