World Cup 2019: England Vs Bangladesh

MATCH DETAILS

England Vs Bangladesh, Match 12 – ICC Cricket World Cup 2019
8th June 2019, 3:00 PM IST

VENUE : Sophia Gardens, Cardiff

DETAILS

The green pitch was on offer for the game between Afghanistan and Sri Lanka and seamers enjoyed the early help from the surface. However, the conditions eased out later on. The teams will be looking to bowl first after winning the toss if the case is same again.

PREVIEW

Both the team are coming from a loss in their previous matches after winning their respective opener contest. England and Bangladesh will be looking for a comeback after the loss. So the match is expected to be a pure thriller.

England stands at 4th position in the points table with 1 win in the two matches they played in the tournament. The team unexpectedly lost against Pakistan in the last match by 14 runs. The positives coming from the last match are Jos Buttler and Joe Root getting a ton.

Bangladesh stands at 6th position in the points table with 1 win and 1 loss in the matches they played. Team's last match was a thriller and Tigers did very well but couldn’t manage to handle their nerves at the last stage of the game.
