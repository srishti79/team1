Selection of Point Guard.

To create the team, you need to select minimum 1 & maximum 4 Point guard.
Selection of taking more or less point guard depends upon the Match and Main Players.
Select the players which are in form by looking at the previous records.
Do not select all point guard from one team. Pick accordingly from each team.
Pick the point guard who’s attacking record is well. This will help to score more points.
Select point guards based on the team performance don't depend on one team. If both the teams are performing well in the series then select at least 1 point guard from each team, if only one team is performing then select more from that team and rest from other team.

Selection of Shooting Guard.

To create the team, you need to select minimum 1 & maximum 4 Shooting guard.
Selection of taking more or less shooting guard depends upon the actual matches attacking lineup. If lineup is strong then select less shooting guards.
Pick at least 2 shooting guard from each team, don't depend on one team.

Selection of Small Forward.

To create the team, you need to select minimum 1 & maximum 4 small forwards.
Select at-least 1 small forward, one from each team and max 4 as small forwards.
Don't select the small forwards who are well known also do a back ground check for his form.

Selection of Power Forward.

To create the team, you need to select minimum 1 & maximum 4 power forwards.
Select at-least 1 power forward, one from each team and max 4 as power forwards.
Don't select the power forwards who are well known also do a back ground check for his form.

Selection of Center.

To create the team, you need to select minimum 1 & maximum 4 Center.
Select at-least 1 center, one from each team and max 4 as centers.
Don't select the centers who are well known also do a back ground check for his form.

Selection of Star Player & Captain (Important)

Selection of Star player and Captain can change the entire game as Star player gets 2 x points and Captain 1.5 x points, so choose it wisely.
