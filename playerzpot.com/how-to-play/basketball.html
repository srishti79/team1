CHOOSING YOUR STARTING 8
To join the game select fantasy basketball squad of 8 players, consisting of :
POINT GUARD
Minimum 1 to Maximum 4 Point Guard.
SHOOTING GUARD
Minimum 1 to Maximum 4 Shooting Guard.
SMALL FORWARD
Minimum 1 to Maximum 4 Small Forward.
POWER FORWARD
Minimum 1 to Maximum 4 Power Forward.
CENTER
Minimum 1 to Maximum 4 Center.
GEMS ALLOCATED (1000)
Select squad within allocated value.
STAR PLAYER
GETS x2 the scored points.
CAPTAIN
GETS x1.5 the scored points.
SQUAD
Maximum 5 players from single team.
But you have to manage your 8 players within the limits of given gems.
BUDGET
The total value of your initial squad must be less than or equal to 1000 gems.
PLAYER PER TEAM
You can create your squad of 8 players from both teams competing against each other.

*
The Star Player will receive 2x the fantasy points for his performance.
*
The Captain will receive 1.5x the fantasy points for his performance.
*
If the captain earns total points in decimal number then we select the upper value of the point.
For EX: If the captain receives 13.5 points we consider it 14 points.
*
No starting points are awarded to any player playing in starting 5 or comes as a substitute.
*
Any event during over-time will be considered for awarding fantasy points.
*
All the players from the winning team who has played in that match will receive a winning bonus.
*
In case a player is transferred/reassigned to a different team between two scheduled updates, for any reason whatsoever, such transfer/reassignment (by whatever name called) shall not be reflected in the roster of players until next scheduled update. It is clarified that during the intervening period of two scheduled updates, while such player will be available for selection in the team to which the player originally belongs, no points will be attributable to such player during the course of such contest.
*
We get our live score feeds for the Basketball fantasy game from the various third party API providers. All fantasy points on the Basketball fantasy game are calculated using, and based on these score feeds only.
*
If a player plays for 30 Min or more, then he is given points for the same.
*
Data is provided by reliable sources and once the points have been marked as completed i.e. winners have been declared, no further adjustments will be made. Points awarded live in-game are subject to change as long as the status is 'In progress' or 'Waiting for review'.
