MUMBAI
Ranking on the second position of points table in all seasons, Mumbai has been scoring great points of 3616 from 111 matches in total. The team has a famous name behind them, Ronnie Screwvala who is founder of UTV Group and owned by Unilazer Sports. Read More >>
PUNE
They have been playing on mid-level in all seasons as they scored 3306 points from 109 matches. Going with the tagline “Khel Badlega” depicts that Game will change. They are owned by Insurekot Sports who are famous for being a big investing factor in sports but this is their first experience in outlay in Kabaddi. Read More >>
BENGALURU
Fully Charged is the tagline Bengaluru use for themselves. They achieved 3392 points from 109 matches in all previous seasons. Their logo, the angry bull represents their ferocious approach. They held the trophy last season gaining the Champions title for the tournament. There’s no celebrity involved in owning this team but the name Uday Sinh Wala,CEO of Kosmik Global Media. Read More >>
GUJARAT
Gujarat marked with 1737 points within 51 matches in total. Their tagline says “Garjega Gujarat” which speaks about the Roars of Gujarat. The entry of this team happened to be late in 2017 in the 7th season. The team is run by Indian Billionaire Industralist Gautam Adani of Adani Wilmar Ltd. Read More >>
JAIPUR
The team owned by another prominent star, Jr. Bachchan. The team has always been in news. They have gained 3271 points from 106 matches in total. Calling themselves as sharp and fast as “Panthers”, Jaipur has they proved themselves Champion in the initial season itself but their performance went low in the following seasons. Read More >>
PATNA
Topping the table of total points in style, Patna holds the record for achieving 3973 points in 115 matches played in all Seasons. They are owned by Co-Chairman & Managing Director, Mukand Ltd., Rajesh V. Shah. The team call themselves “Pirates” who attacks and roves the heights of success. Read More >>
TAMIL
The famous cricketer Sachin Tendulkar owns this kabaddi team. Their motto “Idhu Namma Aatam” which interprets the meaning this is our sport. They have initiated many programmes at grassroot level to widespread the love for kabaddi in the state. They scored 1443 points from 47 matches and they were launched in the 5th Season. Read More >>
TELUGU
Rocking the points table at third position the team achieved 3465 points from 108 matches in all seasons. Veera Sports presently handle the ownership of this team and they are backed by Mahesh Kolli of Core Green group, Gautham Reddy of NED Group and Anil Kumar of Greenko group. Calling themselves the “Titans” meaning the successful people of the field. There spirit describes they work together as one. Read More >>
BENGAL
Going with the slogan #AMAARWARRIORS, Bengal are the team who attained 3182 points playing total of 107 matches in all seasons all together. Their logo, face of tiger signifies the warrior’s spirit of the team. The team is run by famous celeb Akshay Kumar and co-owned by Future Group. The team succeeded to the qualifiers after managing the fifth spot on the league table in the 2018 PKL season. Read More >>
HARYANA
Being the new team on the block emerged in the fifth season, they record total of 1497 points from 47 matches played in two seasons. They are called “Steelers” for the assumed reason of being tough as the metal, unbeatable. The are owned by the renown JSW Group. Read More >>
UP
The team scored 1610 out of 50 matches in all 6 seasons. They are originally owned by the GMR League Games Private Limited. They call themselves “Yoddha” which interprets to be a solider who fights to flourish. Read More >>
