Origin:
Rooting from Pune, this team was founded in 2014 and have their homeground to be Shree Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex, Balewadi, Pune. They have PO Surjeet Singh will lead the team as their captain this season. The team is owned by Insurekot Sports Pvt. Ltd.
Team Coach:
Anup Kumar appointed the new coach for pune is expertise in strategy planning and holds a record of winning the Kabaddi title with U Mumba in Season 2, but has also been the name behind the success of India in the 2016 Kabaddi World Cup. He was the bestowed with the Arjuna Award in 2012 for his gigantic involvement in Indian kabaddi.
