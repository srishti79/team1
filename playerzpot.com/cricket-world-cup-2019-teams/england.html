Origin
The history of England squad started in the year 1739 with the name of ‘All England’. Finally in 1997 the team went on to be governed by the England and Wales Cricket Board(ECB).

    ICC CHAMPIONS TROPHY:
    England has disappointed when it came down in chasing the trophy. they flourished them selves as Runner up in the year 2004 and 2013 held at home ground.
    ICC WORLD CUP:
    the team has also bad experience of World Cup as they have no single victory. they have just qualified as runner up in 1979, 1987 and 1992 against West Indies, Australia and Pakistan respectively.
