ONE -DAY INTERNATIONALS:
  • they are the only team to hunt down 3 sequential tournaments.
  • Australia recorded their biggest victory againstAfghanistan in 2015 world cup by chasing total of 275 runs.
  • Australia played the first ever One Day International in 1971 against England.
TEST MATCH:
  • Australia hold record of approximately 820 test matches out of which they were successive of 386 matches.
  • the team had their major triumph in test match against south Africa by an innings and 360 runs in Johannesburg.
  • their first test match was held in Ashes in the late 1800s and early 1900s.
ICC WORLD CHAMPIONS TROPHY:
  • Australia bagged the ICC trophy twice till date. their first victory was recorded in the year 2006 where the championship took place in India against final opponents, West Indies.
  • Later they chased the trophy in the year 2009 in South Africa as they destroyed New Zealand.
    • ICC WORLD CUP:
  • Aussies emerged winners of world cup for 5 consecutive years.
  • In 1987 they won the tournament against England held in India and Pakistan.
  • In 1999 the ruined Pakistan in England.
  • In 2003 they blew India playing in South Africa.
  • Australia defeated Sri Lanka in 2007 in West Indies.
  • Finally, they shattered New Zealand in 2015 on their homeland to attain their 5 div victory.
