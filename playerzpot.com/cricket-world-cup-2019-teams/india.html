ICC CHAMPIONS TROPHY:
  • India has achieved the trophy twice, once as a joint champion in 2002 in Sri Lanka and sole champion in 2013 in England against the hosts in both the years.
  • they came up as Runners- up in the year 2000 against New Zealand in Kenya and 2017 in England against Pakistan.
ICC WORLD CUP:
  • India kissed the World Cup trophy twice, dating back their first victory in 1983 defeating West Indies in England.
  • Second major triumph was achieved in 2011 ruining Sri Lanka in India.
T20 WORLD CUP:
  • India emerged victorious only once in 2007 in South Africa shattering Pakistan.
  • Later on, in year 2014, India went on to become Runner-Up in Bangladesh as Sri Lanka conquered the trophy.
