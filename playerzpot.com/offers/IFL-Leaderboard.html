Point System – (The contest is only valid for Indian Football League matches from Match 30)

1. Pots joined with an entry fee of Rs. 100 or more will only be eligible for the leaderboard.

2. For every match joined by the user, 6 points will be rewarded. For eg. If you join 2 matches in a day, 12 points will be added towards your leaderboard score.

3. For every pot joined by the user wherever only single squad entry is allowed 2 points will be rewarded towards the leaderboard. 

4. For pots joined by the user where multiple squad entry is allowed, even If multiple entries are made in the same pot only 2 points will be rewarded. 

5. The highest points scored by a user’s best performing team for the match will be added to the leaderboard count. Example: If you join pot/s with 5 teams and the team points are as follows 224,631,488,720 and 108. The highest point from all the joined teams will be considered which is 720 in this case.

To increase your chances of winning, try to score the most points in the matches & join as many pots as you can!
